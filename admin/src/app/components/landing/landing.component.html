How Flyp10 Works

  • 01

    Record

    Record a competition quality performance at open gym, open skate, anywhere.

  • 02

    Upload

    Open the Flyp10 app and upload the video.

  • 03

    Score

    We’ll route it for scoring by a judge qualified in our network.

  • 04

    Share

    Share your score with those close to you.

  • 05

    Have Fun

    View teammate performances, root them on, complete nationally.

Increase feedback, increase development.

Score. Share.

Get a judge’s score, using only your skills and a mobile device.

Upload a recording of a performance or routine, we’ll get you a score and its supporting details from a certified judge in your sport. Build a library of achievements and highlights. Share all of your new scores with your coaches, friends, family and teammates.

Explore our analytics. Track your development and compare against nationwide trends.
