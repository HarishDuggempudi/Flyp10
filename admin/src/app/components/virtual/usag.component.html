How Does It Work:

A USAG Virtual meet runs just like a regular meet with a few extra twists (and hopefully flips).

  • Meets are still registered via the USAG Meet Registration System with the same signup for coaches and gymnasts.
  • Perform in front of the same USAG judges and compete in real-time against other gymnasts while you compete.
  • Here's the twist. You compete at your assigned start-time but from your home gym. Your coach will record your performance and upload it via the Flyp10 app for immediate scoring by judges who are standing by.
  • Scores and recordings of your performance will be available quickly, sometimes before the end of your rotation. Each score will come with judge's notes.

Here are the benefits.

  • Your season goes on when large in-person competitions are limited.
  • All performances (videos, scores and notes) are saved in your Flyp10 account.
  • Flyp10 accounts are all private, but you can elect to share your performances and scores with your coaches, teammates, family and friends.
  • Rolling scores and placement as the competition unfolds, right on your mobile device.
  • Track your performance, competition feedback and individualized feedback all season long, season after season, all in one place.

What do I do?

  • Download the Flyp10 app and open your account (both are free). Add your USAG number to your account profile. Connect your account with your coaches and teammates using the Team Management feature.
  • Enter a into an upcoming USAG Virtual Competition.
  • Get ready to compete locally or across the country and be your best.
